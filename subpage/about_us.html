    OUR STORY

    Emily O’Brien, the founder of Mondo, used medical cannabis to treat the pain from a serious back injury. However, after the back pain was gone, she found that she had a different type of pain: mental pain from a mild anxiety disorder. However, treating anxiety with the same high dose pain relief approach she used for her back prevented her from being productive during work hours.
